Coca-Cola Retailer Sales Analysis Dashboard
Overview:
This project delivers a full business dashboard analyzing Coca-Cola beverage sales across multiple retailers using SQL and Excel. The dashboard highlights core sales trends, key performance indicators (KPIs), and quarterly profit margins with interactive reporting and visual insights.

Dataset"
The data includes sales records for major beverage brands such as Coca-Cola, Sprite, Fanta, Diet Coke, Dasani Water, and Powerade. Retailers involved are Amazon, Walmart, Target, West Soda, and others. Each row contains sales amounts per brand, retailer, year, and quarter.

Key Features & Analysis
Data Cleaning & Preparation:
Used SQL and Excel to clean, standardize, and aggregate retail sales data.

KPI Definition:
Total Sales (2022/2023)
Sales Variance
Sales by Brand
Sales by Retailer

Operating Profit Margin (Quarterly)

Trend Analysis:
Visualized monthly and quarterly sales growth, highlighting brand and retailer performance.

Business Insights:
Easy-to-use dashboard with slicers (Region, Year), supporting business decision-makers.

Visualization:
Combination of bar and line charts to show sales and profit margin trends in Excel.

Tools Used
SQL: Data extraction, aggregation, and metric calculation.

Excel: Pivot tables, dashboard design, slicers, and advanced charting.

How to Use
1.Download the Excel file and sample data.
2.Open the 'Dashboard' sheet for instant business insights.
3.Filter by region or year using interactive slicers.

Business Value
This dashboard helps brands and retailers understand consumer preferences, optimize inventory, and track sales/profit trends to make strategic business decisions.
